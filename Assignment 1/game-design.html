FENN'S GAMES

Game design has always been a dream of Fenn's. He made his first game for his then-girlfriend, which was a short visual novel filled with artwork he had made for her.

His second game focused on the dark sides of OCD and overthinking, and he intends to make many more games in the future.



"Double Check" was a visual novel Fenn started making in highschool, but he never finished it.
